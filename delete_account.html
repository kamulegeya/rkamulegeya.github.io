Account Deletion – Landlord App

Request deletion of your Landlord App account (Email/Password) and associated data.

Delete inside the app (recommended):
Open the app and go to Settings → Delete account.
If you can’t access the app (for example, you uninstalled it), submit a request below.

What will be deleted

  • Firebase Authentication account (your email/password login will be removed).
  • Account-associated app data stored for your account (as applicable in the app).

Processing time

  • Requests are processed within 7 days.
  • If any data must be retained for legal/compliance reasons, it will be retained only as required.

Submit a deletion request (without the app)

For security, we will never ask for your password. We may contact you to confirm ownership of the account email.

Contact (alternative)

You can also email YOUR_SUPPORT_EMAIL with subject Account Deletion Request and include the email used in the app.
