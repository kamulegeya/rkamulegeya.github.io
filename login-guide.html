Landlord App – Registration & Login Guide

Step-by-step instructions for first-time users (Android app).

For first-time users

Getting started

Landlord uses email and password sign-in. If you are new, you must register first, then log in using the same email and password you created.

Create a new account (Register)

  1. Open the app and go to the Secure Login screen.
  2. Tap New user? Register here.
  3. Enter your Email and create a Password.
  4. Tap Register (or Create Account depending on your screen).
  5. After registration, return to the Login screen and sign in.
Tip: Use an email you can access. If you forget your password, you will reset it using email.

Sign in (Login)

  1. On the Secure Login screen, enter your registered Email.
  2. Enter your Password.
  3. Tap Login.

Forgot password?

  1. On the Login screen, tap Forgot Password?
  2. Enter your registered email address.
  3. Check your email inbox for the password reset message.
  4. Follow the instructions in the email to set a new password.
  5. Return to the app and log in with your new password.
If you don’t see the reset email, check Spam/Junk and confirm you typed the correct email.

Troubleshooting

  • “Invalid email”: Ensure the email format is correct (example: name@example.com).
  • “Wrong password”: Use Forgot Password to reset.
  • “Account already exists” during registration: You already registered—go back and log in.
  • No internet / network error: Confirm mobile data/Wi-Fi is working, then try again.
